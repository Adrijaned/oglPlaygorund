◆ changePitch()

Camera & Camera::changePitch ( float  value)

Changes the camera pitch by given value in radians.

Pitch is expected to refer to rotation in up/down manner / along the horizontal axis ortogonal to direction denoted by yaw, with positive numbers used for the up direction.

Warning
Resulting pitch value is clamped to range \( \left\langle-\frac{\pi}{2},\frac{\pi}{2}\right\rangle \)
